What You need to know About KariyaWatch

Kariya Watch is a digital safety platform designed to protect homes and communities by allowing people to report, monitor, and respond to security threats in real-time. Our mission is to help citizens and local responders work together to reduce violence, prevent attacks, and make Northern Nigeria safer for everyone.

  • Real-time Incident Reporting – Users can quickly report crimes or suspicious activity through the app.
  • Live Location Sharing – Share your location during emergencies to get faster help.
  • Security Alerts – Receive instant alerts about nearby threats or attacks in your area.
  • Community Collaboration – Connect with neighbors, vigilantes, and local authorities to improve response.
  • Data for Prevention – Analyze reports to identify danger zones and help plan safer communities.

What do we want to achieve?

Kariya Watch aims to enhance community safety, empower citizens, support local vigilantes and security agencies, promote peace and stability in Northern Nigeria, and use technology to create sustainable solutions to insecurity.
