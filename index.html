Can you help my company get a loan or grant?

Yes! We help ensure your business meets the requirements and presents strong applications.

What is the difference between a Business Name and a Limited Liability Company (Ltd)?

Business Name is ideal for small businesses or sole proprietors and offers simpler registration and lower costs. However, the owner is personally liable for business debts. A Limited Liability Company (Ltd) is a separate legal entity, protecting owners (shareholders) from personal liability, allowing for structured ownership, and making it easier to secure contracts, loans, and government support.

How long does it take to register a business with Tajir Corporate Services Ltd?

The timeline depends on the type of registration. A Business Name typically takes a few working days, while registering an LLC or NGO/Foundation might take a bit longer due to additional requirements and documentation. We always aim for fast, smooth processing.

Can you help my organization get SCUML certification?

Yes! We assist businesses and NGOs in obtaining SCUML certification, ensuring compliance with anti-money laundering regulations, which is essential for handling large transactions and maintaining financial credibility.
